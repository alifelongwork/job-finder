# Elevate Quantum — Company Map

*A reconciled map of the companies in the Elevate Quantum consortium, built from public sources (Elevate Quantum's roster and EDA filings, CU Boulder Venture Partners, company sites, and press coverage). Compiled June 2026.*

## Scope and honest caveats

- This maps the **companies** (the consortium's "Industry / Firms" bucket plus a few firms filed elsewhere). It deliberately excludes universities, national labs, investors, economic-development groups, K–12 partners, and workforce nonprofits — those are listed at the bottom for completeness but aren't employers in the relevant sense.
- **No single authoritative public roster exists.** The consortium spans 120+ organizations across Colorado, New Mexico, and Wyoming; the published lists are partial snapshots. This reconciles the two best public cuts (see below) but the small-startup/advisory tail is necessarily incomplete.
- Two source lists were reconciled:
  - **Phase 1 deck (late 2023):** the EDA pitch deck, which is the only source that lists members *by category*. 70+ orgs; 17 industry firms.
  - **"About" roster (current, ~2025–26):** the alphabetical member list on Elevate Quantum's site. 120+ orgs.
- `*` marks organizations the Phase 1 deck flagged as located **outside** the core CO/NM/WY geography.

## Reconciliation notes (what changed between the two lists)

- **In Phase 1 deck but not in the current "About" roster:** Icarus Quantum, KM Labs, FormFactor, Lockheed Martin, PMG Quantum Advisors (PMG actually appears in both), StratConGlobal, Colorado Technology Association. These are mostly the earlier-cut industry names; some may simply not be re-listed alphabetically rather than having left.
- **In the current "About" roster but not the Phase 1 industry list:** Octave Photonics, Meadowlark Optics, Vapor Cell Technologies, Vexlum, Thorlabs. These reflect ecosystem growth and broader hardware-supplier inclusion since 2023.
- **In both:** Quantinuum, Atom Computing, Infleqtion, Maybell, Vescent, Mesa Quantum, Xairos, Resilient Entanglement, LongPath, PMG Quantum Advisors.
- **Surfaced via CU Venture Partners, not the consortium lists:** Flari Tech (JILA breath-diagnostics spinout) — included because it's a clearly real, active CU Boulder quantum-sensing company in the same incubator ecosystem.
- **Modality reality check:** Quantinuum is the *only* Colorado-headquartered trapped-ion company. Everything else is neutral-atom, photonics/networking, sensing/timing, or enabling hardware.
